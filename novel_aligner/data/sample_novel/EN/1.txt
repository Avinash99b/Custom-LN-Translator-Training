Chapter 1: A New Beginning

"Good morning!"

The morning light streamed into the room. The protagonist woke up, feeling the start of a new day. Today was special - it was the entrance ceremony at the academy.

After getting ready and leaving home, cherry blossom petals were dancing in the wind. Feeling the arrival of spring, the protagonist began walking.

On the way, they met a girl who was also heading to the entrance ceremony. She seemed confused. Was something troubling her?
